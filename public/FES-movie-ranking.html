Frankie's Top 3 movies of All Time

Completely subjective and ever changing list.

The Lion King

"The Lion King" solidifies its position as the greatest animated movie of all time through its exceptional combination of enduring storytelling, awe-inspiring animation, and unforgettable characters. Inspired by Shakespeare's Hamlet, the film delves into profound themes like family, destiny, and the circle of life, ensuring its universal resonance. Enriched by the legendary musical compositions of Elton John and Hans Zimmer, "The Lion King" transports viewers to the captivating landscapes of the African savannah, fashioning an unparalleled cinematic masterpiece that continues to enthrall audiences for generations to come.

Gladiator

"Gladiator" attains on of the pinnacle of cinematics excellence, largely due to Ridley Scott's masterful direction and the breathtaking cinematography that brings ancient Rome to life. Russell Crowe's portrayal of Maximus Decimus Meridius, a betrayed Roman general seeking retribution, serves as a cinematic tour de force. With its compelling narrative, epic battles, and unforgettable characters, "Gladiator" seamlessly melds formal historical storytelling with casual entertainment, cementing its legacy as a cinematic masterpiece.

Saving Private Ryan

"Saving Private Ryan" stands as one of the greatest war movies of all time due to its uncompromising and visceral portrayal of the harrowing realities of World War II. Steven Spielberg's direction and the exceptional performances of the cast. The film's unflinching depiction of sacrifice, camaraderie, and the human cost of war continues to resonate with audiences, making it an enduring classic in the genre.
